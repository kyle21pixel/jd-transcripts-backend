Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you deliver my transcript?

We offer flexible turnaround times from same-day delivery to 5 business days, depending on your needs and the complexity of the audio. Rush services are available for urgent requests.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept all major audio and video formats including MP3, WAV, MP4, MOV, M4A, WMA, and more. If you have a specific format, please contact us to confirm compatibility.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

We maintain strict confidentiality through secure file transfer, encrypted storage, signed NDAs with all staff, and compliance with HIPAA and other industry standards.

What is your accuracy guarantee?

We guarantee 99%+ accuracy on all transcripts. Our quality assurance process includes multiple review stages and experienced transcriptionists specialized in your industry.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing for bulk orders and ongoing partnerships. Contact us to discuss volume discounts and custom pricing for your organization.

Can you handle multiple speakers?

Absolutely! We specialize in multi-speaker transcription and can identify and label speakers throughout the transcript. This is particularly useful for meetings, interviews, and legal proceedings.
