About JD Reporting Company

Professional transcription services with over a decade of experience in delivering accurate, secure, and timely audio-to-text solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, JD Reporting Company began as a small team of dedicated professionals with a passion for accuracy and attention to detail. What started as a local transcription service has grown into a trusted partner for law firms, medical practices, businesses, and academic institutions across Kenya and beyond.

Our founder, John Doe, recognized the critical need for reliable, secure, and professional transcription services in the legal and medical fields. With a background in legal administration and a commitment to excellence, he assembled a team of certified transcriptionists who share the same dedication to quality and confidentiality.

Today, we proudly serve hundreds of clients, from solo practitioners to large corporations, providing them with the accurate transcripts they need to succeed in their respective fields.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, secure, and timely transcription services that enable our clients to focus on what they do best. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality, confidentiality, and customer service in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

To be the leading transcription service provider in East Africa, recognized for our expertise, reliability, and innovative solutions. We envision a future where accurate documentation is accessible to all professionals who need it.

Our Values

Accuracy, Confidentiality, Reliability, and Customer Focus. These core values guide every decision we make and every service we provide, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible experience.
